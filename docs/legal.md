# Legal and Compliance Notes

Tena Forge is designed for authorized material archiving.

Users must only upload files they created, own, or are licensed to use. The service is not positioned as a tool for unauthorized extraction of commercial textbooks, lecture books, paid worksheets, third-party academy material, images, explanations, or diagrams.

## Upload Notice

Recommended in-product copy:

> 업로드하는 자료는 본인이 권리를 보유하거나 이용 권한을 가진 자료여야 합니다. 본 서비스는 자료의 정리, 변환, 아카이빙을 보조하는 도구이며, 무단 복제·배포·판매 목적의 이용을 금지합니다.

## Enforcement

The product records audit logs for important actions, stores source metadata, and can suspend processing or sharing if a rights issue is reported.

## AI Processing

Uploaded files may be processed by configurable AI providers. Enterprise deployments should configure provider retention controls and contractual data-processing terms.
